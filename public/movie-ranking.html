The Best Movies According to Almero

My top 3 Series of all-time.

MR.ROBOT

Great series that has a lot of twist and turns, great characters and one of the best series ever created.

Breaking bad

Great series that has a lot of twist and turns, great characters with very compelling story arcs and one of the best series ever created.

Band of Brothers

A WW2 series that follows Easy company during the war and a bit of the aftermath of the war. It shows how brutal the war really was.
